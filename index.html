Hi, I'm Emily. I'm a designer based in Oakland, CA.

At the moment I'm helping folks take control of their financial lives at YNAB I'm a design manager and I also lead design for the account and transaction experiences on the web, iOS, and Android.

In the past I built tools for creating no-code/low-code in-app experiences at Appcues, worked as design consultant for Fortune 500 companies at Slalom Build, and designed human-guided machine learning tools at Tamr. I have an academic background in cognitive science and human-computer interaction.

Prior to working as a designer, I was a researcher and editor for Sherry Turkle at MIT.
